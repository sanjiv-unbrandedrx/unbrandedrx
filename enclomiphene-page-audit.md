# Enclomiphene Product Page Audit & Competitive Analysis

## Part 1: UnbrandedRx Current Page Audit

### Page Structure
The current page has 6 sections in this order:
1. **Hero Section** - Product image, title, features, pricing tiers, CTA
2. **How It Works** - 3-step process (Online Visit, Doctor Review, Discreet Delivery)
3. **Deep Dive Section** - Brief science explanation (SERM mechanism)
4. **Lab Testing Section** - At-home testing explanation
5. **Content Module** - Enclomiphene vs TRT comparison
6. **FAQ Section** - 8 questions

### Current Messaging Analysis

**Headline:** "Enclomiphene"
**Subheadline:** "A modern, oral medication designed to support your body's natural testosterone production."

**Current Value Props (5 features):**
- Promotes healthy testosterone levels
- Supports your body's own hormone production
- Convenient once-a-day capsule
- Requires recent lab work to establish baseline
- Prescribed by a licensed U.S. provider

**Pricing:**
- Monthly: From $79
- Quarterly: From $189 (20% savings) - "Most Popular"
- 5-Month: From $319 (25% savings) - "Best Value"

**Social Proof:** 4.8/5 stars (no review count)

**CTA:** "Start My Visit"

### Strengths
- Clean, minimalist design
- Clear pricing tiers with savings highlighted
- Comprehensive safety information (collapsible)
- Good FAQ coverage
- Lab testing clearly explained

### Weaknesses
1. **No specific outcome claims** - Competitors claim "1.5-2.5x testosterone increase"
2. **Missing social proof depth** - No review count, no testimonials
3. **Benefit bullets are vague** - "Promotes healthy testosterone" vs specific benefits
4. **No comparison to TRT in hero** - Key differentiator buried below
5. **No credibility indicators** - No doctor credentials, clinical references
6. **Missing emotional benefits** - Energy, mood, libido not mentioned upfront
7. **No urgency or promotional offers** - Competitors offer discounts
8. **"Start My Visit" is weak CTA** - Doesn't convey value

## Part 5: vB Page Structure Recommendation

### Proposed Section Order

1. **Hero** (redesigned with quantified claims, better benefits, testimonial snippet)
2. **Trust Bar** (badges: Licensed Providers, CLIA Labs, Free Shipping, 4.8/5 rating)
3. **Why Enclomiphene > TRT** (comparison table - move up)
4. **Results Timeline** (new section - set expectations)
5. **How It Works** (keep existing 3-step)
6. **The Science** (expand deep dive with mechanism graphic)
7. **Lab Testing** (keep, possibly combine with How It Works)
8. **Testimonials** (new dedicated section with 3-4 cards)
9. **Pricing** (enhanced with comparison anchor)
10. **FAQ** (expanded)
11. **Final CTA** (sticky or bottom section)

### Key Conversion Optimizations

1. **Above-the-fold:** Quantified benefit + social proof + clear price
2. **Sticky CTA:** Mobile sticky "Get Started - $79/mo" button
3. **Exit intent:** "Get $20 off your first order" popup
4. **Trust throughout:** Badges, credentials, review count on every scroll

---

## Summary: Top 5 Changes for vB Test

1. **Add quantified outcome claim** - "1.5-2x testosterone increase" or similar
2. **Add review count and testimonials** - Social proof is severely lacking
3. **Lead with emotional benefits** - Energy, libido, focus, muscle (not just "promotes healthy testosterone")
4. **Move TRT comparison up** - Key differentiator should be above the fold or near hero
5. **Strengthen CTA** - Include price in button, add urgency with promo offer
